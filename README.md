## 🌿 About UniMind

University life is one of the most demanding chapters a person can experience — academically, emotionally, and socially. Yet most students navigate it without consistent access to counseling, structured study tools, or even someone to check in on how they're doing.

**UniMind bridges that gap.**

It's an intelligent mobile app that combines proactive psychological support with practical academic tools, all delivered through a warm, animated AI companion — a Polar Bear that learns your patterns, celebrates your wins, and shows up when you need it most.

UniMind is built *by a student, for students.*

---

## ✨ Features

### 🐻‍❄️ Dynamic AI Companion (The Polar Bear)
The home screen *is* the companion. A fully animated Polar Bear greets you daily, adapts to your mood, surfaces personalized insights, and follows you through the app as a floating avatar when you're deep in a session.

### 😌 Mood & Energy Tracking
Log how you're feeling with a simple daily check-in. The AI analyzes your patterns in the background and proactively surfaces behavioral nudges — not charts.

> *"Hey, I noticed your energy tends to dip on Tuesdays. Want to try a 2-minute breathing exercise before class?"*

### ⏱️ Focus Contract Timer
A distraction-free, full-screen focus timer built on autonomy, not restriction. Set your own goal. After the session, receive a neutral, non-judgmental review:

> *"You stayed focused for 40 of 50 minutes. Instagram interrupted you twice."*

This data feeds back into your mood and insight logs.

### 📊 Academic Confidence Tracker
After every study session or exam, rate how confident you feel about the material (1–5). The Polar Bear uses this to suggest *when* to revisit weak subjects — tying emotional confidence directly to academic pacing.

### 📝 Visual Notes Wall
A cartoon-style digital corkboard for organizing your notes. Create columns per subject, add stylized graphical notes for lessons or thoughts, and customize your layout freely.

### 🧠 AI Study Partner
Upload your course material for intelligent exam prep:
- **Generate Practice Tests** — quizzes or full mock exams tailored to your content
- **Quick Review** — AI summarizes material and highlights expected exam questions

> *Note: This feature is designed for practice and review — not for completing current assignments.*

### 💨 On-Demand Quick Relief
When the app detects signs of stress (low mood logs, long sessions, exam tags), it suggests a **Quick Relief** pop-up. A library of sub-5-minute exercises — including guided box breathing — is always accessible.
